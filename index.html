Clementine Murphy Mixed Media Portfolio:

Clementine Murphy is a 17-year-old artist living in Brooklyn. Clementine focuses heavily on fashion art, with multiple constructed garments, experimental drape work, and textile exploration present throughout her portfolio. Inspired by 80s club kid culture and brutalist architecture, she aspires to study at the prestigious Central Saint Martins, alma mater of influential alumni like Alexander McQueen and Raf Simons.

Favorite Mediums:

  • Oil paint
  • Acrylic paint
  • Mechanical pencil
  • Fabric and sewing
